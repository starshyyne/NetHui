Tell us what you want to see

The programme at NetHui is totally designed by you. We open consultations where you get the chance to let us know what you want to see and hear at NetHui.

Our first survey has been completed where we asked about theming and keynote speaker ideas. We are currently working our way through the feedback.

In a few months we will open another consultation where you’ll have the chance to tell us what specific topics you want to see at NetHui 2017. You’ll also get a chance to let us know if you would like to facilitate a session. Check back here for more information about this process.

Volunteer

There are many options for people who want to be part of the NetHui crew. People can offer to run sessions as facilitators, be a mic runner or help with the collaborative notes for sessions.

Keep an eye out for our next consultation round when you’ll be able to let us know what you want out of NetHui and what you want to be part of. This will open in another few months.
