Programme

The programme for NetHui will not be live until later on in the year. This is because the programme is set by you - the community who want to attend.

Our first consultation has been completed where we asked about theming and keynote speaker ideas. We are currently working our way through the feedback.

In a few months we will open another consultation where you’ll have the chance to tell us what specific topics you want to see at NetHui 2017 and suggest a topic you might like to run a session on. You’ll also get a chance to let us know if you would like to facilitate a session. Check back here for more information about this process.
